Live Fresh Cold Pressed Juice

Cold pressed juice is extracted from fresh produce by slowly separating the fiber from the cell to retain almost all of its nutritional content.

Here at Live Fresh, we believe that consumers have the right to expect healthy options in a market full of fast food, while balancing daily responsibilities and routines. Creating healthy food options creates better communities and is a crucial part of the balance of growth and well-being of our community.

Hours: Tuesday-Saturday 12PM-8:PM; Closed Monday
Category: Food, Smoothies
Payment methods: credit card, cash

Address: 114 E. 8th Ave., Homestead, PA, 15120
